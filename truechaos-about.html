↳ Our Origin

Est.
2017

True Chaos was born from a simple frustration — too many agencies were building safe work for brave brands. We decided to do it differently: put creative courage first, strategy second to none, and results above all.

2017
2019
2021
2024
Now

We were tired of watching bold brands get watered-down creative. So we built a studio where the work actually matched the ambition.

It started in a small Lagos studio in 2017, with a handful of clients who trusted us to push past the brief. Within two years, we had built a reputation for work that didn't just look good — it moved people, drove revenue, and made brands impossible to ignore.

Today, True Chaos is a full-service creative and marketing studio working with brands across Africa and beyond. We've shipped campaigns that went viral, identities that redefined categories, and strategies that opened new markets.

We're still that same scrappy, obsessive team from 2017 — just with sharper tools, deeper expertise, and an even stronger conviction that great work starts with the courage to be different.
